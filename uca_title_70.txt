Utah Code Annotated § 70-3a-101 Title.
--------------------------------------
This chapter is known as the "Registration and Protection of Trademarks and Service Marks Act."

Utah Code Annotated § 70-3a-102 Relation to federal law.
--------------------------------------------------------
Utah Code Annotated § 70-3a-102(1): This chapter shall be interpreted to provide for the registration and protection of trademarks and service marks in a manner substantially consistent with the federal system of trademark registration and protection under the Trademark Act of 1946, 15 U.S.C. Sec. 1051, et seq.
Utah Code Annotated § 70-3a-102(2): In interpreting this chapter, a construction given the Trademark Act of 1946, 15 U.S.C. Sec. 1051, et seq., should be used as persuasive authority.
